      MAYO  SANDWICH

  Ingredients:-
1.Mayonnaise
2.Bread
3.Cucumber {circular slices}
4.Tomato {circular slices}

                         Recipe
Step 1- Take bread and apply mayonnaise on it(according to your taste).
Step 2- Do the stuffing of cucumber and tomato.
Step 3- Take another bread and apply mayonnaise to it.
Step 4- Put them all together.
